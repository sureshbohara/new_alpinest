Everest Region

Greetings, and welcome to the Alpinist Club Adventure!

The Alpinist Club Team comprises experienced mountain guides and enthusiasts who have been organizing treks and expeditions in Nepal for over a decade. Our team has successfully summited Everest and other challenging peaks, driven by a strong passion for adventure. As a mountaineering club, we bring together people who love climbing and outdoor adventures. We promote rock climbing, ice climbing, trekking, and peak climbing. We organize trips, expeditions, and training programs to help members improve their skills and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow adventurers.
